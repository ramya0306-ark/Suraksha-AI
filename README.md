# Suraksha-AI  
It is a smart women’s safety system that uses AI and location analysis to predict area risk and generate safety scores. It includes live GPS tracking, emergency contacts, and an SOS alert feature, providing real-time monitoring and proactive protection in critical situations.

Key Features:
👋 Welcome Page-Introduces the platform and explains the purpose of the women safety system.
🏠 Home Dashboard-Provides quick access to all safety tools and important features.
🔎 Safety Checker-Uses an AI model to predict the risk level of a location and generate a safety score.
📍 Hotspot Map-Displays crime hotspot areas on an interactive map, helping users identify unsafe locations.
📝 Raise Complaint-Allows users to report safety issues or incidents.
📂 Complaint History-Users can view their previously submitted complaints.
📊 Complaint Analysis-Shows analysis of complaints to understand safety patterns and trends.
👥 Emergency Contacts-Users can save trusted contacts who will be notified during emergencies.
🚨 Emergency SOS-An SOS button that sends alerts and location details to emergency contacts.
👤 Profile Management-Users can manage their personal details and safety settings.

Model uses an Artificial Neural Network (ANN) to analyze crime-related data and predict the safety level of a location.
Risk levels are classified as:
🟢 Low Risk
🟡 Medium Risk
🔴 High Risk
Based on the prediction, the system generates a Safety Score to indicate how safe the area is.
